About Charlotte

Art as a gift —
given and shared

I'm a self-taught artist, Berry College student, and sports enthusiast from Shelbyville, TN. My art journey started with pencil sketches and homemade cards — "Charmark" cards, as I called them — and grew into oils, acrylics, and a love for painting portraits that bring real joy to people.

"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others." — 1 Peter 4:10

My faith is at the heart of everything I create. I believe we are all made in the image of a creative God, and that art is one of the most beautiful ways we get to reflect that.
